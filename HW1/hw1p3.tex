Semijoin $R \leftsemijoin S$ of two ternary relations $R$ and $S$

\begin{enumerate} 
  \item Relational algebra
  \begin{equation*}
    \pi_{R.A,R.B,R.C}(\sigma_{R.B=S.B \land R.C=S.C}(R \times S))
  \end{equation*}

  \item SQL
  \begin{verbatim}
    SELECT R.A, R.B, R.C 
    FROM R, S
    WHERE R.B = S.B AND R.C = S.C
  \end{verbatim}
\end{enumerate}
